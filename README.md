# Byteling 🥚

> Every machine has a daemon. Byteling lets you meet yours.

Byteling is a desktop creature **born from your hardware**. Your CPU, GPU, RAM, and
machine age determine the species that hatches — and from then on, its mood and health
are a living reflection of your actual PC. Hot GPU? It runs a fever. Disk full of junk?
It gets scruffy. Thirty days without a reboot? It can barely keep its eyes open.

Caring for your Byteling *is* caring for your machine.

## Status

🚧 Early development — pre-v1. Follow along via [Issues](../../issues) and the
[roadmap](docs/ARCHITECTURE.md#11-roadmap).

## Principles

- **No fake stats** — every mood traces back to a real sensor reading
- **Local-first** — zero telemetry; your pet never phones home
- **Lightweight** — a pet that slows your PC down is a failed pet
- **No admin rights** required (v1)
